Your comprehensive lifetime environmental credit is ...

Your carbon credit is your net worth and your safety net when you retire climate neutral. When you retire, your comprehensive lifetime environmental credit will be available for you to transfer to your wallet. Then, you can exchange your carbon credit for things you need in order to stay climate neutral. Your comprehensive lifetime environmental credit is deducted from this number by smart contracts that exchange your credit for other things you need, at the rate of exchange that is estimated using hard science without conflict of interest.

To transfer your credit to your wallet, connect to your wallet.
